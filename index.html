How It Works

Three steps to reduce image size — no account, no upload, no waiting.

1

Load your image

Drag & drop, paste from clipboard (Ctrl+V), or click Upload. Supports JPEG, PNG, and WebP input files of any size.

2

Choose quality & format

Use a preset (Web, Photo, Email, Lossless) or fine-tune the quality slider. Switch between WebP, JPEG, and PNG output formats. The before/after comparison updates instantly so you can judge quality loss at a glance.

3

Save or copy the result

Download the compressed file directly or copy it to your clipboard. All client-side image compression runs entirely in your browser — nothing is ever uploaded.
